What this is

An AI-powered planning and outreach engine for reaching pharmaceutical manufacturers. The operator opens a conversation with the tool to game-plan a campaign: picking which manufacturers to target, what angle to lead with, what signals to surface. The system pulls live signals from Clay and six federal drug databases, identifies the right contacts inside each manufacturer (Medical Affairs, Commercial, Market Access leads), and writes a tailored email per contact, grounded in real signals about their company. Every email passes deterministic verification at the code level before the operator sees it.

Tested across nearly 50 manufacturers and hundreds of generated emails during development. Current working set: 96 verified emails at a 97% pass rate. Sales has reviewed the content over the past two weeks; feedback drove revisions, and the first batch is greenlit for live sending.

What good looks like

Outreach grounded in real manufacturer signal, persona-targeted to each contact, brand-consistent with Impiricus voice, produced in seconds and verified before it reaches the operator.

Why this exists

The general-purpose outreach stack can be pointed at pharma. It can be loaded with attachments, slide decks, and product context. But there's a ceiling to what that approach reaches. The differentiation lives in the boxes below.

Pharma context loaded, not bolted on
Other tools accept pharma context as runtime input: slide decks, product briefs, persona notes the model consults. Here it's structural: every prompt, scoring decision, and verification check is written in pharma from the ground up.
Agentic planning inside HubSpot
The operator opens a conversation, points the tool at HubSpot, and works through which manufacturers to target. The tool prospects inside the CRM and builds the campaign.
Open signal stack, composed per campaign
Most outreach platforms ship a fixed enrichment menu. This one isn't capped by a vendor's catalog. Any data source available in the wider API world can plug in. Today: Clay enrichment plus six federal pharma sources. Tomorrow: whatever the campaign needs.
Layered verification, not a single LLM call
Scoring, signal validation, fabrication guards, content gates, and a final 10-check code-level pass. Claims that fail the gate never reach the operator.
